Mushroom, Run!

About This Project

This project is a very simple game made using SFML libraries. In the game, you play as a mushroom trying to destroy zombies (which later turn into golems) approaching from both sides of the level by jumping on top of them. If an enemy collides with the player from the side, the player loses.

The scoring system adds one point each time the player kills an enemy. However, if the player kills multiple enemies before landing on the ground again, they are given one extra point incrementally. On killing one enemy in a chain, the player is awarded +1 point, on killing a second, they are awarded +2 points, on killing a tenth, +10 points. There is no limit to this if the player can keep the chain going.
